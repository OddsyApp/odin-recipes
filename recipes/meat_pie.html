Meat Pie

A meat pie is considered an Australian classic. Whilst the best meat pie should come from your local bakery, it is possible to make them at home. Let's take a look below at what you'll need.

Ingredients required:

Here are the main ingredients required for this recipe. You can find these at your local supermarket:

  • Pie crust base
  • Meat pie filling
  • Pie topping

Steps:

  1. Lay your pie bases out on a baking tray.
  2. Add your meat pie filling to each base, ensuring an even amount in each base.
  3. Place pie toppings on each prepared pie.
  4. Preheat your oven to 180℃ and cook for 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
