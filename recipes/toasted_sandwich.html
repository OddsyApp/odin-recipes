Toasted Sandwich

The toasted sandwich is a staple in any household. It's perfect for lunches or dinners, and you can practically put anything you want on them. This recipe will show you how to make the best toasted sandwich; one you'll probably want to make again and again.

Ingredients required:

Here are the main ingredients required for this recipe. I am keeping the recipe simple with ham and cheese, however you can add what you like. You can find these at your local supermarket.

  • Bread (any kind of bread will work for this)
  • Fillings (e.g ham, bacon, cheese, egg)
  • Butter
  • A sauce of your choice

Steps:

  1. Butter two slices of bread, on one side of each slice.
  2. Lay one slice of bread butter-side down and add your fillings.
  3. Add sauce if preferred.
  4. Place the sandwich onto a sandwich press. Ensure the butter side is making contact with the press.
  5. Add the second slide of bread, butter-side up so that it makes contact with the top side of the press.
  6. Allow the sandwich to toast until golden brown.
