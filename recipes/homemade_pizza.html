Homemade Pizza

Who doesn't love a good pizza? Whilst it is customary to buy a pizza from your local shop and sit down with a few drinks on a Friday night, we all know that can get expensive. Here, I'll show you how to make one at home; same quality, for a fraction of the price.

Ingredients required:

Here are the main ingredients required for this recipe. You can find these at your local supermarket:

  • A pizza base (any base will work)
  • Pizza sauce
  • Your chosen toppings (e.g cheese, ham, pepperoni)

Steps:

  1. Lay the pizza base out on a pizza tray.
  2. Smother the base with pizza sauce. It's best to use a spoon here.
  3. Add the cheese and ensure the entire pizza is covered.
  4. Add toppings to the pizza. The more, the better. Remember, this is your pizza, and nobody can take that away from you.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180°C.
  6. Place the pizza in the oven and cook for 10-15 minutes or until cooked to your preference.
